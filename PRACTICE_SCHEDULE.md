## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Record all practice sessions**
   - You improve faster seeing yourself
   - Catch bad habits early

2. **Practice in the actual room (if possible)**
   - Reduces anxiety
   - Test acoustics
   - Know the space

3. **Bring backup everything**
   - Slides on USB
   - Screenshots
   - Quick Reference printed
   - Phone hotspot (if demo needs internet)

4. **Dress well**
   - Professional appearance = credibility
   - Comfortable shoes (you'll be standing)
   - One step above classmates

5. **Energy management**
   - Coffee OK, but not too much (jitters!)
   - Light breakfast
   - Hydrate throughout morning
   - Bathroom break before

6. **Use the "pause" technique**
   - After key points, pause 2 seconds
   - Let information sink in
   - Shows confidence
   - Gives you time to think

7. **Make it conversational**
   - "You might be wondering..."
   - "Here's what's interesting..."
   - "Let me show you..."
   - Not robotic lecture!
